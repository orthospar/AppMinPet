Reading Ternary Diagrams

import {ternarySlider} from '@yurivish/ternary-slider'

pc = d3.format('.0%')

viewof Composition = ternarySlider({
    value: [0.3, 0.2, 0.5],
    labels: ['MgO+FeO', 'Fe2O3', 'SiO2']
})

The pointer indicates a composition of:

  • MgO + FeO,
  • Fe2O3,
  • SiO2
